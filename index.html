Tips for Financial Cybersecurity
Protecting your money online is very important in today’s digital world. Cybercriminals often target bank accounts and payment applications. Always use strong and unique passwords for financial accounts. Enable two-factor authentication for extra security. Avoid using public Wi-Fi while accessing banking services. Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords with anyone. Be careful of fake emails, links, and phishing scams. Regularly check your bank transactions for suspicious activity. Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches. Safe online habits can help protect your financial information and money..
